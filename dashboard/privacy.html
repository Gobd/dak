Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

This dashboard is a personal kiosk application. It connects to Google Calendar to display your events.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays in your browser:

  • Google Calendar access: Used only to display your calendar events. Your OAuth tokens are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • Dashboard configuration: Your layout preferences are stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

Third-Party Services

This app uses:

  • Google Calendar API: To read your calendar events. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • National Weather Service API: To display weather data. No personal information is sent.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to external servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser's developer tools.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer at bkemper@gmail.com.
